## Power Supply Requirements

**Q: What PSU should I use?**  
**A:** Required specifications: 19V / 10A with 5.5-2.5mm DC connector.

**Recommended Models:**

- **Germany:** Innergie 180G ([Amazon.de link](https://amzn.to/4ddbg5C))
- **UK:** JHZL Laptop PSU ([Amazon.co.uk link](https://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/B09FNTBGYJ))
- **Universal:** Meanwell LRS-350-24 PSU ([Amazon link](https://amzn.to/3X6A2O3))

!!! warning "Meanwell LRS-350-24 Assembly Required"
    Meanwell LRS-350-24 requires custom enclosure and DC cable assembly. Suitable for powering multiple devices (Rhino joystick + FFB pedals). See [wiring video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dO298h9jgdc) and [TRC-LRS-350 Enclosure](https://trcelectronics.com/products/enclosure-trc-lrs-350).

**DC Connector Quality:**

High-quality DC connectors are critical. Low-quality no-name connectors from Amazon may fail under amperage loads. Invest in quality components.

**Recommended Connectors:**

- DC connector cable: [Amazon link](https://amzn.to/48veznb)
- Fork cable lugs (smallest red): [Amazon link](https://amzn.to/40yE5px)

!!! tip "Share Your PSU Experience"
    If you find a reliable PSU that works well, please share your feedback with Kaltokri to help future customers.

## Assembly and Complexity

**Q: Is it complicated to assemble?**  
**A:** No. Kits designed for straightforward assembly:

- **Skill level:** No soldering required. Anyone with basic mechanical skills can assemble.
- **Time required:** One evening (3-4 hours typical)
- **Pre-assembly:** Most complex components (Rhino Joystick kit) pre-assembled by vendor

**Required Tools:**

- Crimping pliers and wire end ferrules for VPforce motor kit ([example tool](https://www.amazon.de/dp/B0BXL6Q455))
- Slotted screwdrivers
- Metric allen key set
- Small TORX driver (for mainboard)
- Basic tools (knife, etc.)

!!! tip "Assembly Assistance"
    If you encounter problems during assembly, contact Kaltokri via email or VPforce Discord for assistance. Parts fit tightly but should not require excessive force.

## Warranty and Returns

**Q: Do I have warranty or right of return?**  
**A:** No. These are DIY kits with the following characteristics:

**Nature of Product:**

- DIY kits with procured materials and custom 3D-printed parts
- 3D printing done by Winger or Kaltokri in PETG (durable material)
- 3D-printed parts cannot match injection-molded plastic quality
- Parts assembled and printed to order

!!! warning "No Warranty, No Returns"
    Warranty, return, and exchange are excluded due to custom assembly nature. Vendor provides support for questions and problems that arise.
